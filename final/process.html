Wireframes and Mockup

Here are two wireframes for the main homepage and a project page. I wanted to keep things fairly simple and flexible for the wireframes. Below the wireframes is a mockup of what the homepage would look like. I use the color red in a lot of my branding on the internet so my white and red color scheme is carried over into this site as well. I love typography and had some fun with Google Web Fonts for this design. I think the clean and finished design encompasses my taste and design aesthetic well.

Card Sorting

My Card Sort

I want to do an extensive personal site for my final project; something that is professional and displays my work, experience, and more. I want something for my portfolio and some place I can create a blog and post my process. My sort was similar to the other sorters, except that I place the portfolio and blog on the Home page. I wanted to highlight social media and resources as its own page to show my involvement in the design community. I want to create an responsive HTML resume with an option to print out a designed PDF version. Contact and About are fairly standard pages that I thought I needed, but might end up moving that to the footer or something else.

Other's Card Sorts

The three people I had do the card sort of my personal website had fairly standard layouts of a personal portfolio website. Some had the portfolio on the Home page, while another had it separate. Similarly, most grouped Experience, Resume, and About together and had the Blog on its own page. This sort was easier than the other because there is less information.
